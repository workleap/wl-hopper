- Added new `Chip` and `ChipGroup` components for filtering and multi-selection scenarios.
- A `Chip` must be rendered inside a `ChipGroup`; rendering one outside throws an error.
- Defaults `selectionMode="multiple"` to match the typical filtering use case.
- Added `comp-chip` branding tokens for Workleap and ShareGate (color, border-radius, typography).
